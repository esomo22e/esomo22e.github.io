Mutual-Aid Maps

For this project, we wanted to document mutual-aid efforts, that have been showcased on twitter. We scraped with mutual aid and community donation data on twitter using key words such as 'mutual aid', 'funds', and 'donations' to gather mutual aid efforts for various social causes. On the left, we showcased where each mutual aid is located and on the right we count how many mutual aid funds per state, witht he size of the circle being represent the amount. Also, click each circle to see the tweet.
