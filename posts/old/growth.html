Digital Growth

I created a generative art piece that is controlled by the click of the mouse. The premise of this piece was to use particles to show a sense of growth. The digital growth is developed with a Particle class, which randomly decides the length, direction, and twist of the particles in order to create a 3D affect. I wanted to use the mouse to display and show increasing growth.

Role: Interactive Designer and Developer

Tools: Processing
