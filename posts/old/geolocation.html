How companies use geolocation data to target you

A long-form scrollytelling project about how companies target their consumers using geolocation data.

I was brought into the project as a designer to assist fellow developers, illustrators, and reporters on how to organize the images within the story. I was given images to organize and design a visual story that allows for users to visually engage with the story and to coincide with the written work. Furthermore, it gave me the opportunity to learn how to utilize responsive design to develop digital projects.

Story: How companies use geolocation data to target you

Role: Digital Designer and Developer

Tools: Handelbar.js, CSS (Stylus)
