Water Networks

Inspired by the Boston Globe’s story Flooding could shut down Logan airport, hospitals, and other critical infrastructure in Massachusetts, and the risk is only growing. I made my Boston Water Atlas, which is a data warehouse to meet the needs of of local government, into a zine about flooding in the area. I want to identify flood hazards and how it could possibly affect communities and neighborhoods in Boston.

Role: Strategy, Photographer, Designer

Tools: QGIS, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator

Data: FEMA Data, Social Vulnerability Data/p>
