E-Motion Movement

E-Motion Movement is the third installation in a series of interactive installation inspired by hip hop culture. This installation is a digital representation of Breaking/B-boying, and my intentions to use movement/dance to be visually represented and expressed.

Role: Interactive Designer and Developer

Tools: Microsoft Kinect, Kinect Libraries in Processing
