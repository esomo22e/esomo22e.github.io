Tangible Graffiti (Part 2)

Second Part of Tangible Graffiti which “capture bubbles” of each frame of the webcam. The Bubbles are dependent on movement so the more you move the bigger the bubbles and it capture the colors of the frame. I feel like this captures the larger than life aspect of graffiti and it makes the view feel that the piece is coming alive.

Role: Interactive Designer and Developer

Tools: Microsoft Kinect, Kinect Libraries in Processing
