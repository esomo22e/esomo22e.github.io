Trump Quotes from around the world

I was asked to design and develop a data visualization project showcasing President Trump quotes from all over the world that was gathered by reporter, Matt Rocheleau from the Boston Globe. I wanted to use D3.js, Google sheets, and CSS to allow Boston Globe readers to visualize the amount of countries, if they were positive or negative, and what was said about those countries. I used design thinking, data analysis and visualization to make this applicable for both web and mobile.
