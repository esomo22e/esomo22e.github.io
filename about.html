ABOUT

Interactive Designer, Installation Artist, and Creative Technologist that focuses on using various tools and mediums to build participatory experiences to answer the question at hand.

I hold a Bachelor of Arts from Mount Holyoke College where I developed an interdisciplinary major called Interactive Design and Development, which integrates computer science, art studio, and design thinking.

My goal for my projects is to explore questions that I have through different materials, ranging from cloth to data, in order to process information and tell stories. Using those tools and fusing a variety of materials allows me to explore various themes and find answers.

My recent work involves working with data and exploring ways to visualize data through various mediums, both digitally and physically, to approach ways to communicate a diverse range of ideas. Overall, my work allows me to try to visualize and understand issues facing marginalized communities.
